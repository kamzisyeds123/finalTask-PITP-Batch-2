About MUET

Learn about our history, vision, and mission

Our History

Mehran University of Engineering and Technology (MUET) was established in 1963 as the Sindh University Engineering College. It was upgraded to a university in 1976 and named after the late Mr. Allah Bux Soomro, who laid the foundation of the campus. Today, MUET is one of the leading engineering universities in Pakistan.

The university has produced thousands of engineering graduates who are serving in various sectors both nationally and internationally. MUET is known for its strong emphasis on research, innovation, and practical learning.

Vision

To be a world-class institution of higher learning in engineering, technology, and science, contributing to the socio-economic development of Pakistan through excellence in teaching, research, and innovation.

Mission

  • To provide quality education in engineering, technology, and science
  • To foster a culture of research, innovation, and entrepreneurship
  • To produce graduates with technical competence, ethical values, and leadership skills
  • To collaborate with industry and community for sustainable development
  • To promote diversity, inclusion, and equal opportunity
