Academic Departments

Explore our departments and academic programs

MUET offers a wide range of academic programs through its various departments. Each department is committed to excellence in teaching, research, and innovation.

Civil Engineering

The Department of Civil Engineering offers undergraduate and graduate programs focusing on structural engineering, geotechnical engineering, transportation, and environmental engineering.

Learn More

Electrical Engineering

Offering programs in power systems, electronics, telecommunications, and control systems. The department has state-of-the-art laboratories for practical learning.

Learn More

Mechanical Engineering

Focuses on design, manufacturing, thermal sciences, and automotive engineering. The department has strong industry collaborations for student internships.

Learn More

Computer Systems Engineering

Offers programs in software engineering, computer networks, artificial intelligence, and cybersecurity. The department emphasizes hands-on learning and innovation.

Learn More

Chemical Engineering

Focuses on process engineering, materials science, and environmental engineering. The department has research collaborations with national and international institutions.

Learn More

Architecture

The Department of Architecture offers a comprehensive B.Arch program focusing on design, urban planning, sustainable architecture, and heritage conservation.

Learn More

Industrial Engineering

Focuses on optimization of complex processes, systems, and organizations. The program covers operations research, supply chain management, and quality control.

Learn More

Basic Sciences

Provides foundational courses in mathematics, physics, and chemistry for engineering students. Also offers graduate programs in applied sciences.

Learn More

Research Centers

MUET also hosts several research centers that foster interdisciplinary research and innovation:

Center for Energy & Environment

Conducts research on renewable energy, energy efficiency, and environmental sustainability.

Center for Advanced Materials

Focuses on development and characterization of advanced materials for engineering applications.

Center for Information & Communication Technology

Works on cutting-edge ICT solutions, software development, and digital innovation.
