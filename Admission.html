Admissions

Apply to MUET for undergraduate and graduate programs

Join MUET Community

MUET offers a wide range of undergraduate, graduate, and doctoral programs in engineering, technology, and sciences. Our admission process is designed to identify talented and motivated students who will thrive in our academic environment.

Admission Process

1

Check Eligibility

Review the eligibility criteria for your desired program. Requirements vary by program and degree level.

2

Submit Application

Complete the online application form and upload required documents through our admission portal.

3

Entry Test

Appear for the MUET entry test (for undergraduate programs) or submit required test scores (for graduate programs).

4

Interview & Selection

Shortlisted candidates will be called for interview. Final selection is based on multiple criteria.
